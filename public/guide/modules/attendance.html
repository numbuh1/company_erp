4 Attendance

Record your daily presence — either On-Site at the office or Working From Home (WFH). Each day you can check in once.

Check-In Types

TypeHow it worksApproval
On-Site Click the On-Site button. The system validates your IP address against the configured office network. Records 8 hours automatically. Auto-approved
WFH Enter your planned hours and a reason, then submit. A notification is sent to your team leader or supervisor for approval. Pending approval
💡
If your administrator has granted you WFH without approval, your WFH submissions are approved instantly — no waiting required.
⚠️
On-Site check-in is only available when connected to the company network. If your IP is not recognised, you will see an error message. Use WFH instead if you are working remotely.

Checking In — Step by Step

On-Site:

  1. Ensure you are connected to the office Wi-Fi.
  2. Go to Attendance in the sidebar.
  3. Click Check In — On-Site.
  4. Your attendance is recorded immediately as 8 hours.

WFH:

  1. Go to Attendance in the sidebar.
  2. Click Check In — WFH.
  3. Enter your planned hours and a reason.
  4. Click Submit. Your team leader or supervisor receives a notification.

WFH Approval Flow

After you submit a WFH request:

  • Your team leader(s) receive a bell notification with your name, date, hours, and reason.
  • If you have no team leader, the notification goes to your supervisor.
  • The approver visits the Attendance page and clicks Approve or Reject next to your record.
  • You receive a bell notification when a decision is made.

WFH Request Statuses

StatusMeaning
PendingWaiting for approval from your team leader or supervisor
ApprovedWFH confirmed
RejectedNot approved — a rejection reason is shown

Team Attendance Overview (managers)

Users with the module attendance permission see a stats panel showing a head-count for each category across their team or the whole company:

CategoryDescription
On-SiteChecked in at the office today
WFHWFH approved for today
WFH PendingWFH submitted but not yet approved
On LeaveHas an approved leave request covering today
Not Checked InNo attendance record and not on approved leave

With view all attendance, you see all active users. Otherwise, you see your team members and direct reports.

Permissions

PermissionAccess Granted
module attendanceAccess the Attendance page and view team/company stats
view all attendanceSee all active users in the stats panel (not just team)
approve attendanceApprove or reject any WFH request regardless of team
ℹ️
Without approve attendance, you can still approve WFH requests from users in teams you lead or from your direct reports.
